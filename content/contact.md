Public Layer Lab can be contacted by email:

publiclayerlab@gmail.com

You can use this address for questions about the work, source suggestions, possible conversations, corrections, or accessibility and privacy concerns.

If you are working on a public-facing or socially relevant digital or administrative system and want to discuss questions of access, explanation, accountability, challenge, or repair, you are welcome to get in touch. This is an invitation to conversation, not a standing offer of legal, compliance, certification, or technical audit services.

Public Layer Lab is the public-facing name of Stichting Instituut voor Digitale Publieke Systemen, based in ’s-Hertogenbosch, Netherlands.

If you contact Public Layer Lab by email, the information you provide will be used to read, manage, and respond to your message.

More information is available on the [Privacy page](/privacy/).
