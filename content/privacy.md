This website is operated by Public Layer Lab, the public-facing name of Stichting Instituut voor Digitale Publieke Systemen.

This is a small static website. It does not use analytics cookies, tracking cookies, advertising cookies, newsletter tracking, contact forms, comments, or embedded social media widgets.

## Contact by email

If you contact Public Layer Lab by email, we may process the personal data you provide, such as your name, email address, organisation, role, and the content of your message.

We use this information only to read, manage, and respond to your message, and to keep appropriate records of correspondence where necessary.

We do not retain correspondence longer than necessary to respond to your message, manage the correspondence, or meet applicable administrative or legal obligations.

## Hosting

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical data, such as IP addresses and usage information, as part of providing, securing, and operating GitHub services.

## Cookies and analytics

This website does not currently set analytics cookies, tracking cookies, or advertising cookies.

If this changes in the future, this page will be updated before such tools are used.

## Third-party services
